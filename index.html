About Stronghold A3

From tactical operations to strategic risk management, we are Eastern Visayas' most trusted security partner.

Our Strategic Advantage

Stronghold A3 Security Agency represents a new standard in security service delivery. Unlike traditional guard suppliers, we provide comprehensive zero-liability security solutions backed by decades of regional expertise and strict adherence to Philippine regulatory standards.

Operating throughout Tacloban, Leyte, Biliran, Samar, and Eastern Visayas, we understand the unique challenges our region faces—from tropical weather patterns to socio-political dynamics. This local knowledge translates into proactive threat mitigation rather than reactive responses.

Under the leadership of CEO Antonie M. Silva, Stronghold A3 has developed a robust operational framework that combines human expertise with cutting-edge technology. Our personnel undergo rigorous training aligned with DOLE Department Order No. 174 series of 2019, ensuring every team member meets national certification standards.
